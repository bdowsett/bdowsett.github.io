Meet our Team

We have a fantastic small team of friendly, passionate beer lovers working at Mortimer Brewery. At the moment we have 6 full time members of staff, including our two founders, as well as a number of fab part time and casual staff in a variety of roles from accounts to events. As a team, everyone mucks in when required, and whilst we each have our individual responsibilities, the overall Mortimer Brewery lies in the hands of every individual. We are proud of our values and Mortimer Brewery wouldn’t be what it is without our incredible team and how they go about living them every day.
